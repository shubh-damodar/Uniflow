Your global valve partner

Uniflow control instruments Pvt. Ltd. (UCIPL) is a pioneer of control valve manufacturer providing customized solutions to the process industries.
Today for more than a decade UCIPL has been offering their expertise globally to all major process industries namely Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Fertilizer, Chemicals, Pharmaceutical, Paper, Cement, Steel & Power, Sugar Industries.
UCIPL is recognized for its superior product quality and has been associated with leading engineering consultants like Thyssenkrupp industrial solutions (formerly UHDE India ltd), Jacobs, Tata Consulting Engineers (TCE), Aker Solutions, Mott MacDonald, Mecon - All a pioneer in the field of Engineering worldwide.
